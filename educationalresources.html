Educational Resources

The Montreal Chinese Hospital is committed to providing access to evidence-based educational resources to support our patients, families, and caregivers. These resources can help you better understand and manage various health conditions related to aging and cognitive health.

Frailty Resources

Canadian Frailty Network (CFN)
Comprehensive resources on frailty in older adults, including prevention and management strategies.

AVOID Frailty Brochure
Learn practical strategies to prevent and manage frailty through this evidence-based resource.

Dementia and Cognitive Health

McGill's Dementia Education Program
Access comprehensive dementia education resources developed by McGill University experts.

Dementia: Your Companion Guide
A free, downloadable guide available in multiple languages including Simplified and Traditional Chinese and Tagalog (Filipino).

Brain Health and Aging

Brain Health PRO
Created by experts in brain health and aging, this program provides essential tips on maintaining cognitive health as you age.

Federation of Quebec Alzheimer's Societies
Find support, resources, and information about Alzheimer's disease and related conditions.

Caregiver Support

Quebec Government - Informal and Family Caregivers
Official provincial resources and support for informal and family caregivers.

Caregiver Support Helpline
A personalized counselling, information and referral service for caregivers, families, health professionals and employers.

For additional information about these resources or if you need assistance accessing them, please contact our Social Services department at 514 871-0961, extension 240.
